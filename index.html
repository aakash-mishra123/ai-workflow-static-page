Project Challenges: Balancing Precision, Security & Adoption in Legal Practice

Successfully implementing AI solutions across 100+ attorneys required careful attention to legal ethics, data security, and attorney buy-in while maintaining the highest standards of confidentiality.

Confidentiality Risks

CHALLENGE

Client data exposure could violate ethics rules.

SOLUTION

Implemented on-premises AI with zero-data retention and with HIPAA/GDPR-compliant encryption.

Attorney Resistance

CHALLENGE

Skepticism about AI replacing legal judgment got the best out of the lawyers.

SOLUTION

We allayed their fear by co-designing tools with senior partners and preserved human oversight at all stages.

Legacy System Integration

CHALLENGE

Critical data lived in siloed databases.

SOLUTION

To overcome this challenge, we built API-first architecture syncing with Clio & NetDocuments.
